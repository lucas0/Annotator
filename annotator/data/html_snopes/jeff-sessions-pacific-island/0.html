You can download podcasts a number of ways:

    1. Subscribe to the podcasts through Apple Podcasts
    2. Subscribe to the podcasts through Google Podcasts
    3. Subscribe to the podcasts through Google Play
    4. Subscribe to the podcasts through Stitcher
  1. Use this RSS feed to subscribe to the podcasts through your favorite podcatcher
  2. Look below for the list of available podcasts you can stream or download
  3. Follow the directions below:

Click on this RSS feed link to open it into a new tab (if youre using the Chrome browser, youll have to first install Chromes RSS Reader plug-in):

  1. Find the date of the podcast you want to download and click on it. A new page will open and the podcast will start.
  2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page and right-click on the Download button.
  3. In the pop-up menu, select Save Link As
  4. Choose where on your computer you want to save the podcast
