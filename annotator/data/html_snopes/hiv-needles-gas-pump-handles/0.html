It's a Hoax!


June 20, 2000

If you're inquiring about a warning from a Capt. Abraham Sands that HIV-infected hypodermic needles have been affixed to gas pump handles in Jacksonville, it's a hoax.

The Jacksonville Sheriff's Office has had no reports of such incidents and there is no "Capt. Abraham Sands" at the JSO.

Please notify the person who emailed you that it is an email hoax.
