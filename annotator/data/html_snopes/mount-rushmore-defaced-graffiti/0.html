Welcome to Keystone, South Dakota!

EarthCam and the National Park Service have partnered to deliver exclusive webcam views of Mount Rushmore. Dedicated on October 31, 1941, the 60-foot tall faces of Washington, Jefferson, Lincoln and Roosevelt gaze upon the 3 million annual visitors from 500 feet above.
