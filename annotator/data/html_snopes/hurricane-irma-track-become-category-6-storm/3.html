michael on the issues

Abolish The Income Tax
Lowering tax rates is not nearly enough. Eventually, I would like to completely abolish the individual income tax and shut down the Internal Revenue Service. The greatest era of economic growth in all of U.S. history was when there was no individual income tax, and I believe that we can eventually return to those days.
Abortion / Planned Parenthood
​​Since Roe v. Wade was decided in 1973, nearly 60 million babies have been murdered in America. I am 100% Pro-Life, and I will work tirelessly to end abortion in America. If I am elected, I will use the full power of my office to go to war with Planned Parenthood.

About Michael

​I am not a professional politician. For the last seven years, I have been touching millions of people all over the planet through my websites, my books, my videos and my frequent radio and television interviews. My wife and I truly love our very quiet life in Bonners Ferry, but now we feel called to try to fight for the future of this country.  I have a two-year-old daughter named Atarah, and I often wonder what the future is going to hold for her. Because if America stays on this current path there isn't going to be any sort of a positive future for this nation. What we need is a "second American Revolution".  It won't be a revolution with guns and bullets, but rather one of ideas, values and principles. Every generation of Americans has had to fight for liberty and freedom, and now it is our turn.
