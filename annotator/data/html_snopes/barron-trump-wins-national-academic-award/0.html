About

The Daily Chronicle was founded in 1987 as a way to fight back against biased and inaccurate mainstream media sources. It started as a project by University of Kansas undergraduates in their parents’ garage. Taking a few pointers from William F. Buckley and The National Review, The Chronicle has been striving to deliver the kind of news that Conservative minded news consumers are in dire need of: the truth.
With reporters from all 50 states and 23 different countries, The Daily Chronicle is here. All About The Facts, Not Feelings.
