F. McNeill and Co of Patent Felt Works, Bunhill Row, London, E.C.; of 52 Russell Square, London, WC1.

Patent Slag Wool.

1833 Company established.

1911 Manufacturer of Felts for the Railways.[1]

1914 - Advert - McNeill's Lion Brand Dampcourses, Bunhill Row, London.

1937 Manufacturers of felt and insulating materials. "Lion" Felts and Slagwool. "Slagbestos" Slagwool.
