Candy Stallings  SAN BERNARDINO, CA
San Bernardino Sexual Assault Services is a non-profit organization providing services to survivors of sexual assault and their families. Our services include: a 24/7 crisis hotline, crisis response and intervention, advocacy, accompaniment, individual and group counseling, community prevention education, and art healing workshops. All of our services are available at no cost to victims, their significant others, and family members.

We have seven locations throughout our service area. Office locations include: San Bernardino, Redlands, Yucaipa, Victorville, Barstow, Morongo Basin, and Coachella Valley 

Our mission is to provide a safe place for survivors to find healing and support.

Volunteer opportunities are available.
