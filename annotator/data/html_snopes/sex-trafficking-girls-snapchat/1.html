Snap Map FAQ

Have any questions about how Snap Map works? Check out some answers to frequently asked questions below!

How do I view Snap Map?

To open the Snap Map, go to the Camera screen and pull down 👇

You can also view Snap Map on the web!

When will my location be shared on Snap Map?

Your location won’t be visible on Snap Map unless you choose to share it with friends when you set up Snap Map. After that, your location will only be shared with your friends who you’ve chosen to share your location with, and will only be updated when you have Snapchat open.

When you share your location with friends, you'll see Snap Map information in Profiles, too.

What are the 4 location settings?

When you first set up Snap Map, you’ll be prompted to choose between one of the four privacy settings below:

Ghost Mode (Only Me)

When you’re in Ghost Mode, your Bitmoji will hold up a blue sign with a 👻 icon. You can turn Ghost Mode on and off, or set a timer if you only want to be on the down low for a little while. Your location won’t be visible to anyone else on the Map! Keep in mind that Snaps you submit to Our Story can still show up on the Map, no matter what location setting you choose. Your location won’t be visible to anyone else on the Map!

My Friends

Your location will be shared with all of your friends — including friends you add in the future! This option doesn’t include people who’ve added you as a friend, but who you haven’t added back. If you choose this option, we’ll check in from time to time to make sure you still want to share your location with all of your friends.

My Friends, Except…

Your location will be shared with all of your friends, except the friends on this list 🚫

Only These Friends…

Choose specific friends to share your location with! Friends you select aren’t notified when you choose them. If a friend isn’t appearing in this list, it could be because they haven’t added you as a friend on Snapchat.

Can everyone see my location on Snap Map?

Only friends you’ve chosen to share your location with can see where you’re at on Snap Map. Learn how to change who you’re sharing your location with.

How long does my location stay on Snap Map?

Your location will disappear from Snap Map after several hours, or as soon as you go into Ghost Mode. You can also set a timer if you only want to be on the down low for a little while.

Will a friend be notified if I tap on their Bitmoji?

Friends will not be notified if you tap on their Bitmoji. Tapping on their Bitmoji just lets you start a Chat and see how long it’s been since their location was last updated! However, they will know if you view their recent Explore Activity.

Can I disable Snap Map?

Once you’ve set up Snap Map for the first time, you can’t disable it altogether — but you can always hide your location from other Snapchatters by going into Ghost Mode.

Do you have any tips for keeping my privacy safe when using Snap Map?

To make sure you’re staying as safe as possible, we’d suggest:

  • Only choosing to share your location with people you know
  • Checking in on your privacy settings often to make sure you’re sharing your location how you want to
  • Only submitting things to Our Story that you don’t mind other people seeing, including street signs or landmarks that can indicate your location
