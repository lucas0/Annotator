Greyhound Racing: Rightfully Banned in 40 States

It begins when they are separated from their mothers at breeding farms, extends into physical and medical neglect, and all too often ends in injury on the track, abandonment, or death. What do the owners do with injured and losing dogs?

Rescues take greyhounds no longer wanted on the track, but the cost of neglect & injury is prohibitively high.
