Macon County, Missouri Sheriff's Office
11. Dezember 2015 ·

After further investigation, the Macon County Sheriff's Office has learned information regarding the multiple cell phones being purchased at the Macon Walmart. It is believed these purchases were made by a cell phone trafficing ring. The cell phones are not believed to be purchased for use in criminal activity. This type of trafficing business usually results in civil action between the cell phone trafficker and the cell phone carrier company. Typically what happens is cell phones are purchased in a region for a low price and shipped to another region and sold to the general population for a large profit. This type of activity happens with everything from baby formula to electronic equipment. It is not believed there is any direct connection to violent criminal activity. More information can be found at www.stopcellphonetrafficking.com
