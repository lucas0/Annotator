About Us

Bongani General Trading LLC (Bongani Dubai) was established in Dubai, United Arab Emirates in 2009 as a Freight Forwarding company. Bongani Dubai falls under the umbrella of Bongani Investment Holdings (Pty) LTD an investment holdings company incorporated in 2004. As the holding company for Bongani group of companies. The group has developed an extensive network of business relationships and this coupled with their market intelligence know how and skills, manage projects and companies that are financially viable and sustainable since Bongani’s inception, the company has growned to be recognized as a diversified business with interests in various sectors such as General Trading, Real estate and Construction, Waste Management, Forestry, Heavy Equipment. 

Bongani Investment Executives have combined experience of many years in Business entrepreneurship, and finance and investment management in Africa and around the world.

Subsidiaries of Bongani Group

  • Bongani Investments and Consultants
  • Bongani resources
  • Bongani properties
  • Bongani Industrial and manufacturing

 

Bongani general trading LLC is one of the market leaders in the field of Freight Forwarding, Shipping and Logistics.
