Las Vegas Shooter Reportedly a Democrat Who Liked Rachel Maddow, MoveOn.org and Associated with Anti-Trump Army

October 2, 2017 by Jim Hoft 118 Comments
  • Total: 0
  • a0Share
  • d0Tweet
  • vEmail
Guest post by Joe Hoft
Geary Danley, the murderer of at least 20 concertgoers in Las Vegas, from initial accounts was a far left loon.  Per a review of his Facebook account he was a fan of Rachel Maddow, People’s Action, Democrats, MoveOn.org, etc…
 
 
Everipedia.com reports that Geary was another angry far-left shooter –
Geary is a fan of the The Rachel Maddow Show​ and liked several pages on Facebook​ including Thank You Obama, Anti-Trump Army, Progressive Day, Organizing for Action, Not My President, Proud to Be A Democrat, Fight Trump, Boycott All Things Trump, and Impeach Trump. Geary is a Democrat and is registered to vote in Washoe County, Nevada​.
According to other reports, Danley was married to Marilou Danley, an Asian American from the Philippines.
Her husband Geary is a fan of the The Rachel Maddow Show​ and liked several pages on Facebook​ including Thank You Obama, Anti-Trump Army, Progressive Day, Organizing for Action, Not My President, Proud to Be A Democrat, Fight Trump, Boycott All Things Trump, and Impeach Trump.
Geary opened fired on the 34th floor of the Mandalay Bay toward a concert happening across the street. Police stormed his room and shot him dead.
