Usain Bolt Scandal: Girlfriend Reveals She’s a Man!

 0
By NLM on December 15, 2016 Entertainment, Sports
One of the world’s most famous athletes, Usain Bolt , is now experiencing a whole new kind of attention. His self-proclaimed girlfriend, Kasi Bennett made a shocking revelation that she was actually born a boy! Her name at birth was David Roy Fitch and she lived the first half of her life as a man, until she opted to have gender corrective surgery in her early 20’s.
Bennett says that she was born ‘intersex’ and claims that at birth she had male genitalia with no testicles, but she also has a uterus and no ovaries. “No one can say they knew me as a man or produce a photo of me as a man,” she posted on Facebook.
Kasi Bennett
Bolt, 30, allegedly met Kasi Bennett ,  on Tinder back in August and later invited her over to watch a Soccer game. She asserts that the pair became extremely intimate during half-time and “the intimacy with him was amazing! It was the first time in my life that someone has made me feel like a true woman,” she said.
Bolt, who did  not know about Bennett true sex, is in for a shocker when he is expected to leave rehab later this week and finds out his girlfriend announced to the world she was born a man. Bennett says “she’s unsure if their relationship will continue.”
