The new ANTIFA flag by Linumhortulanus

After hearing about the terrible revolts of far-left extremists in Hamburg (this year's location of the G-20 summit) I felt like I had to make them a new... and more appropriate flag! It's inspired by the national socialist war ensign of Germany. It's supposed to symbolize that the extremism of far-left organizations like the ANTIFA is the same... or rather... as harmful as the extremism of far-right movements. I also added a molotov cocktail. It represents the brute force this organization uses against the property of the state or of civilians (cough... the rioters set fire to civilian's cars... cough). I think the violence against the policemen which were trying to protect regular people and politicians from all over the world from these brutal leftist riots is also a possible interpretation of this molotov cocktail.

I think that far-left extremism is a big danger for the people of Germany. I made this to remind everyone that organizations like the ANTIFA are just as harmful and violent as neonazi gangs. My thoughts are with the brave policemen who did their best to keep this masses of rioters under control. Many of the officers were injured after all. The rioters made Hamburg look like a battlefield! Fire and destruction everywhere! The fact that the far-left party with the fitting name "The Left" and the despicable nonsense-party of the Greens defended these criminals... and blamed the police for the escalation off the "protest"... makes me furious!
