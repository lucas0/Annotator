BREAKING NEWS: Singapore club shut down for killing patrons and selling as meat

The Singapore Police Force has shut down a nightclub, Touches Nightclub, in Singapore after investigation uncovered that the owner, a foreigner (nationality withheld) killed patrons of the facility and served to others as meat.
The Police said in a statement that they acted swiftly upon tip-off and a search at the club revealed human parts kept in deep freezers.
The Touches Nightclub started operating in February 2019.
The statement added that the owner of the facility has been arrested and other three people believed to be accomplices are at large.

The statement assured the general public that the situation has been uncovered and as such patrons can go ahead patronizing other clubs and report anything suspicious to the Police.
The statement went ahead to welcome whistle-blowers and promised to hide their identity and ensure maximum security for them.
