Biography

Thomas Miller is a postdoctoral research associate at King’s College London and currently holds a PhD in Environmental Toxicology (KCL). Previous taught degrees include a MSc in Analytical Chemistry and a BSc(Hons) in Biology. His interdisciplinary research is currently focussed on the impact of environmental micropollutants on aquatic wildlife. Dr Miller is currently a named co-investigator on a BBSRC Industrial Partnership Award (IPA) held with AstraZeneca and the Francis-Crick Institute (ref: BB/P005187/1). The research will focus on the development of high-throughput screening methods for the analysis of pharmaceuticals and their impact on the metabolome and phenotypic traits.
