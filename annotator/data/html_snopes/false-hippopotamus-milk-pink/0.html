Hippo milk

4th Jan 2012 11:05:44
  • Question from:Jan Geestman

I've heard that hippo milk is pink.
Is this true?
And if it is, why?

5th Jan 2012 14:48:57
  • David Wynick
  • University of Bristol
  • Posts: 4,230

I think this is an Internet legend that is oft repeated but without any evidence for it that I can find.

It is true that hippos secrete a red pigment in their sweat that acts as a natural suntan lotion (see URL below), but nowhere can i find evidence it is secreted in breast milk and thus turns it pink. Further, since the pigment is acidic I would guess that would not combine well with the milk.

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hipposudoric_acid
