1) AUTHOR BY PLANE

The author's concept behind the publicity campaign was to bring some of the characters from his book to life in cyberspace on the net. The response has been overwhelmingly supportive, as hundreds of thousands of people around the world have been following the campaign, wondering where it would lead ... This entire project was specifically designed to create publicity for the release of his novel.

For his first publicity event the author chartered a small plane to fly him over the Dandenong Mountain Rage in Victoria, Australia. This was to get the photos that the character HM would use to bring to life the 8march2003.com site.

 

HM of www.8march2003.com

2) DAMAGED CAMERA

The character HM plays a small, but vital role in the first book. The experience and knowledge he gained while on the run from the faction, serves as a major reason for what he has achieved five years later, in 2008. The author purposefully posted the original page as a typical 'tabloid' style presentation, and inserted clues and discrepancies throughout the piece to indicate its fictional nature. This allowed the reader to pick up any inconsistencies and to make comments about them, in turn making it interactive. This influenced the way HM's story developed. Many of the emails that HM received during the campaign, are posted on his site.

 

The www.mountainsub.com site

3) SUBMARINE IN THE MOUNTAINS

This site was set up by the faction that was chasing HM, to undermine his posting on March 8 2003. You may like to read HM's story on his site leading up to this date, before you view the mountainsub.com site.

4) THE MEDALLION

Professor Winwood claims to have found an ancient medallion in Egypt, that he believes was connected to the mysterious and fabled civilisation of Atlantis. The medallion plays an important role in The Shift of the Ages series. And remember: Professor Winwood's story will continue during the next few weeks. So keep dropping in to find out the latest news as the professor discovers astounding revelations about the medallion's purpose and its prediction for humanity. Further clarity will come, as the full story unfolds in the book.

The Author had a master jeweller create this exquisite and mysterious medallion for this particular publicity site.
