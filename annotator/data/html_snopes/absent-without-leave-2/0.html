IRINA MALENKO: 4,000 ISRAELIS WORKING ATTHE WTC FAILED TO TURN UP AT WORK ON THAT DAY

 After it was announced that the WTC in New York had been attacked, the mass media of many countries, especially in Israel, hurried to avail themselves of the incident and declared mourning for 4,000 Israelis working in the towers of the World Trade Centre. All of a sudden, the Israeli workers stopped being talked about. It became clear later that they did not turn up at work that day when four passenger jets were hijacked in the USA and used in terrorist attacks. Nobody is talking about the Israelis being killed or wounded in the attack. According to Arab diplomatic sources cited by the Jordan newspaper Al-Wattan, the Israeli workers just did not appear at work that day, because they had taken a ⌠hint■ from Israeli secret police (Shabak). This fact caused concern among American officials who want to know how the Israelis knew of the attacks before they attacks were carried out and why the Israelis did not advice the US authorities about it?
Suspicions became even stronger after the Israeli newspaper Yadiot Akhranot came out with the news that Shabak allegedly prevented Premier Sharon from visiting New York (specifically, to the eastern part of the city) to attend a festival organized by Zionist groups to rally ⌠support■ for Israel. Akharon Berni, the newspaper▓s commentator, raised this question, but did not get any answer. He even requested information as to what were the grounds of warning Mr. Sharon, and again he received no answer. Mr. Berni went on to say that Premier Sharon was very enthusiastic while making an address at the festival and asked the leadership of the host organization to persuade Shabak that there was nothing to worry about. The next day after Mr. Sharon▓s secretary announced that his boss would not attend the festival, the WTC went tumbling down.
The Israeli newspaper Na-Arets, in its turn, says that the FBI arrested five Israelis when they were filming the smoking rubble from their office▓s roof; those Israelis are being accused of ⌠enigmatic behaviour.■ It is also said that they were rejoicing and even sneering while filming.

IRINA MALENKO
