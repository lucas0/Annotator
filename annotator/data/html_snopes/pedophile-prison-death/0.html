Pedophile Who Raped Baby Found Dead in Cell with Penis Chopped Off

Pedophile was part of sick gang that filmed them raping babies

James King, 31, who changed his name from Robin Hollyson, was jailed for 24 years for raping a three-month-old baby and filming it.
A fellow prisoner, John Denham, confirmed to the inquest that inmates at HMP Bristol took contacts on King.
King was part of s sick gang that took delight in drugging and raping babies described by a judge as “evil beyond rational understanding”

"James suggested to us that the attack on Matthew was intended for him. I believe he was concerned and I believe he was told about the attack by another prisoner."
Prison workers were called to Kings prison cell to at 9:20 pm on January 21 to find King unconscious with his penis chopped off.
Operational support worker Francesca Sage was the first to arrive but she did not open the cell door fearing it was a staged attack.
Within ten minutes she was joined by three other prison workers and two nurses who performed CPR on King.
King was taken to Southmead Hospital but died two days later.
