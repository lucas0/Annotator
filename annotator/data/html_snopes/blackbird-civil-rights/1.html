Paul McCartney

Hosted by Chris Douridas May. 25, 2002
Tune in to NewGround this Saturday for a very special Memorial Day weekend treat: a conversation with rock superstar Paul McCartney. Host Chris Douridas recently caught up with the former Beatle on the Dallas stop of his Driving Rain tour. Paul tells us about the new album, candidly discusses his career from the Beatles to the present, and gives us insight into the inner workings of his songwriting process.
