Dataset:Crime in England and Wales: Appendix tables

26 April 2019

For year ending March 2018 data only - North Wales Police inadvertently reported a number of historical crimes to the Home Office which were included in this dataset. Revised figures can be found in more recent publications of this dataset (those released for year ending June 2018 onwards).

Contact:
Meghan Elkin

Release date:
25 April 2019

Next release:
18 July 2019

About this dataset

Trends in Crime Survey for England and Wales (CSEW) crime experienced by adults and children aged 10 to 15 yearsand Home Office police recorded crime, by offence type. Also includes trends in offender relationship of CSEW violence.
