Meet Mukhande of Fountain Of Truth Spring Water

VoyageLA Staff
Share
Tweet
Pin
Today we’d like to introduce you to Mukhande.
Mukhande, let’s start with your story. We’d love to hear how you got started and how the journey has been so far.
A few years ago, I was shocked to discover all filtered and even bottled spring water gets sterilized and irradiated for cheaper transport and shelf stability. Similar to juice and dairy products that are pasteurized to save costs. This destroys all the natural probiotics found in raw spring water. Our bodies thrive from these, and they aren’t found in other food sources. It’s why many people just don’t feel compelled to drink enough water.
This inspired me to seek water existing in its natural form. The first time I drank fresh living spring water a surge of energy and peacefulness entered my being. I could never go back to drinking dead water again.
I had a neighbor whose new born baby wouldn’t latch to her breast. Doctors told her it was from mineral deficiency. This mineral deficiency was caused by drinking reverse osmosis filtered dead water. It was actually stripping her body of nutrients. I shared some raw living spring water with her and that enabled her to breast feed. That’s when I knew people living in metropolitan areas needed access to this ancient source of all life.
I started journeying to a spring on Big Bear Mountain, to gather water for sharing with all my friends in LA. Back then I just had a hybrid electric SUV, wooden trailer, and some 5-gallon glass jugs. So many people were excited to receive raw water that word quickly spread, and the original spring couldn’t keep up with the demand. That’s when I set out to find the best spring water available for the community. I drove and hiked the entire West Coast for nearly a year till I was certain I discovered the ultimate source
Opal Spring levitates out of 108,000 gallons per minute. It originates from super deep where there’s no chance for industrial age contamination. It’s so pure the town of Madras Oregon has been drinking it raw for over 50 years. It’s abundant in the primary electrolytes, the liquid crystal mineral silica, and at least 5 living water exclusive recently discovered probiotics.
Even BPA free plastic bottles are cancer forming. Recycling requires wasteful energy and resources. Traditional 5-gallon glass jugs were hard to handle and dangerous for many people. So, we designed our own reusable glass system. Every single piece of glass we sell is handmade and slightly unique. Our flower of life jugs and dispensers are compact, easy to maneuver, and designed to celebrate the magic of water. I also wanted to design the perfect travel size re-usable glass bottles. Our reusable 1 liter orb and 1 gallon globe bottles are easy to wash and effortless to carry with their insulated organic cotton bags. They have glass caps so there’s no metal or plastic interference.
Our mission is to provide convenient access to fresh living spring water for the whole planet. We are committed to donating 10% of profits to a dropinthebucket.org. which provides wells throughout Sub-Saharan African.
We’ve already established a convenient carbon neutral delivery system, and are excited to share with more people. We also sell our empty 2.5-gallon glass jugs, for anyone who wants to go collect spring water themselves.
I’m optimistic that we can all have access to pristine water, and learn to share it with each other. I’m excited to be part of the collective evolution towards co-operation over competition. Through our forces combined all water will return to perfect health and vitality. What a great day that will be.
We’re always bombarded by how great it is to pursue your passion, etc. – but we’ve spoken with enough people to know that it’s not always easy. Overall, would you say things have been easy for you?
Raw Living Spring Water is by far the best thing you can drink. This is a new idea for most people, but it’s actually the most ancient consensus, as humans drank this for 99% of our existence. Processed water has only been on the scene for about 100 years. It’s certainly an obstacle to change most of the Western’s world’s ideas about water. It also goes against all current industry standards. The way we transport water in refrigerated glass has never been done before. There has been LOTS of challenges to overcome. The fun part is solving those challenges.
We’d love to hear more about your business.
We are super proud to be the first company to offer fresh living spring water in refrigerated glass jugs.
What were you like growing up?
I never fit in at school. I refused to conform to the public school education designed for making robots. I was always in trouble, and they tried to medicate me. Thank god this inspired me to seek a life closer to nature and how our eldest ancestors lived.
