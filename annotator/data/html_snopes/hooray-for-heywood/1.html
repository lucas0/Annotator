Wonder how The Simpsons got the ideas of Bart's prank calls? In the mid 1980's prank call bootlegs (aka the Tube Bar Tapes) circled around America. The tapes were created by two guys named John Elmo and Jim Davidson (aka the Bum Bar Bastards) who would document their calls to a bar in Jersey City, New Jersey named the "Tube Bar". Asking for people like, "Al Coholic" and, "Ben Dover", the bartender, Red, would pick up the phone and ask aloud for these fictional people. -Information provided from The Simpsons Folder
