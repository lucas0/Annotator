Center for Health Promotion

Castle's Center for Health Promotion offers an array of health education and lifestyle enrichment resources to keep Windward families healthy and fit. Classes, activities, events and health screenings are provided free or at a nominal charge. These include:

  • Coronary screening
  • Cholesterol screening
  • Nutrition classes
  • Weight management classes
  • CPR classes
  • Exercise classes
  • Dance classes
  • Seniors Programs
  • Vegetarian cooking classes
  • Smoking cessation programs
  • Personal nutrition and fitness counseling
  • HealthVan Services

The Castle Center for Health Promotion is located at 41-006 Kamehameha Highway in the Castle Professional Center.
