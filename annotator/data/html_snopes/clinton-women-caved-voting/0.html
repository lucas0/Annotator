  • Former US Secretary of State Hillary Rodham Clinton said that US President Donald Trump has a bromance going on with Russian President Vladimir Putin.

    Donald Trump has a liking for dictators, said Hillary Clinton at the India Today Conclave in Mumbai today.

    Talking about the alleged Russian interference in the US election and the campaign against her, Hillary Clinton said that as a US Secretary of State in 2011, she had said that people of Russia deserved free and fair elections.

    She said that Putin blamed her for people protesting on the streets in Russia and he was very upset about it. Clinton said that she was also outspoken about Russia's role in Syria.
