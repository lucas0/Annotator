Around the turn of the century, Mr. and Mrs. Johnson had a son. He was a healthy baby, but was unable to properly digest breast milk. The assistance the Johnsons needed to help their son was not available as there were no baby formulas at the time, until Edward Mead Johnson, Sr., a chemist, saw the need and began to develop an alternative for breast milk patterned closely after breast milk. Since the beginning of the 20th Century, Mead Johnson has pioneered research and development efforts in the field of nutrition. Today the company provides quality assistance to mothers that enables them to make the best nutritional choices for their babies. Our researchers continue to analyse mothers milk, from its nutrient composition to its immunological properties, and use that information to guide in the continual development of what is known today as the Enfalac Family of Formulas.

ENFALAC FAMILY OF FORMULAS

Both Enfalac & Enfalac Iron Fortified provide high quality nutrition for your baby's growth and development. Both are formulas that meet the nutritional needs of most babies. If you are not breast-feeding, the Canadian Paediatric Society recommends the use of an iron-fortified formula for up to the first year of a baby's life.

ENFALAC AR

 Spitting up after feedings may not be easy on your baby. Enfalac AR is clinically proven to help babies who frequently spit up after feedings to experience fewer feedings followed by spitting up, and less associated discomfort than babies fed routine formula*. Enfalac AR is an iron fortified, cow's milk-based formula, thickened with added rice starch.  

ENFALAC LactoFree

Enfalac LactoFree is for babies with common feeding problems associated with lactose intolerance such as mild diarrhea, gas, and general fussiness. It has the appearance and taste of regular infant formulas, but Enfalac LactoFree replaces the lactose with a different, easier to digest carbohydrate.

ENFALAC PROSOBEE SOY

Enfalac ProSobee Soy is a soy-based formula that is used when an alternative to milk-based formula is needed (i.e. galactosemia, vegetarian diet). Enfalac ProSobee Soy is a lactose-free, sucrose-free, and iron-fortified formula.

ENFALAC NUTRAMIGEN HYPOALLERGENIC

Enfalac Nutramigen is a hypoallergenic formula that provides relief of colic caused by milk protein allergy. It's been specially designed for babies with food allergies or sensitive to whole proteins. Enfalac Nutramigen is iron-fortified, contains a readily digestible form of protein (hydrolyzed casein) and is an excellent alternative for babies with food allergies.

ENFALAC NEXT STEP

Enfalac Next Step is a transitional formula for babies six months and older who are starting on solid food, but aren't ready for cow's milk. Cow milk's low levels of iron - and high levels of protein make it hard for babies to properly digest. Iron is very important for your growing baby, and cow's milk can actually reduce iron absorbed from other foods in your baby's diet. That's why cow's milk shouldn't be introduced into your baby's diet until after the first year. Enfalac Next Step has been specially formulated to fulfill your baby's nutritional needs and is gentler on your baby's digestive system than cow's milk.
