SUBSCRIPTIONS 

If you've surfed around our web site and liked what you saw, or if you're a frequent visitor to Lancaster's Pennsylvania Dutch Country, or if you just like the articles, perhaps a subscription is for you!

Amish Country News is printed 7 times per year --- Spring, June, July, August, September, October and Holiday. Annual subscriptions (7 issues) are $30 (available only within the United States). A subscription also makes a nice gift.

TO SIGN UP:

Simply send a check or money order and this form to:
Amish Country News Subscriptions
P.O. Box 414
Bird-in-Hand, PA 17505

Don’t forget to include your name, address, phone number (optional) and starting issue.
