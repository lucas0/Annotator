Trump Military Transgender Ban Allowed by the Supreme Court

The latest striking news, at least for thousands of trans in US, comes from the Supreme Court today. President Trump proposed to limit the trans for being in service in the military. By the latest update, this ban was voted and is already allowed to be applied. Still, controversy is ongoing.

– The Transgender Policy
In 2017, Trump tweeted that the country will no longer allow Americans transgender to serve in any military service anymore. The statement was then further explained by Jim Mattis the defence secretary. This rule is applied to transgenders whose identity and biological sex don’t match or those who have gender dysphoria history.

Currently, there are at least 9,000 American transgenders in active duty. This policy was mentioned not something they need to be concern of. agen bola continued by stating that the policy shouldn’t apply to those who are willing to serve on their biological sex.

This policy has been the contrary of what Obama made before. On the previous policy, American transgenders are allowed to serve in military. More than that, they have the right to obtain special funding for surgery of gender re-assignment. Of course, this is a shocking news with what Trump further request to the Supreme Court.

– What Trump Wants with This New Transgender Policy?
The Trump administration already sent a request to lift any injunctions that block the transgender policy. The Supreme Court put it into vote and resulted in 5-4. Meanwhile, the battle still continues in the lower court. At least four liberal judges decide to oppose the new policy ruling.

As the policy clearly prohibit American transgenders who have or need surgeries to serve in US military, Trump claimed it as disruption and tremendous costs of medical procedure. However, the Supreme Court decision is never a mandate for the lower courts.

However, the ruling should allow several parties to actually enforce the ruling. If not all, the Trump administration requested some more expedited ruling for the discussed policy, yet the Supreme Court declined to process it. However, this can’t be the ending. If they can enforce the new policy, we know what they are trying later.

The lower courts still refuse to apply the policies, thus create frustration on the behalf of Trump administration. They asked the judges to step in for some more clarifying discussion. While Trump administration finds the policy helpful for US military effectiveness, it is a backward movement for the country for so many people.
