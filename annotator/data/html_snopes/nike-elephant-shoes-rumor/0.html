About- Who is PMK?

PMK Customs, formerly know as Pimp My Kicks,  is not you ordinary run-of-the-mill custom sneaker company but yet- a custom and handmade goods company specializing in sneaker collaborations, sneaker reconfigurations, and most recently contemporary sportswear. PMK Customs the parent company of a family of exclusive brands including the eponymous PMK, uber exclisive Dangerous Gentlemen, and the contemporary shoe line Xception. Visit http://www.pmkcustoms.com to purchase exclusive PMK merchandise. With PMK, nothing is safe. Get hip or get lost…
