Incautados 40 kilogramos de cocana en buque de Liberia Seized 40 kilograms of cocaine in ship from Liberia

Tamao actual: 100% Current size: 100%
Fecha: Date:
Lunes, Agosto 25, 2014 - 10:30 Monday, August 25, 2014 - 10:30
Unidades de la Armada Nacional en desarrollo de una operacin coordinada con personal del CTI de la Fiscala seccional de Magdalena, incautaron 39.8 kilogramos de clorhidrato de cocana a bordo del buque de transporte de carbn de nombre Ping May y de bandera de Liberia. Units of the National Navy in development of a coordinated operation with personnel of the CTI of the Magdalena branch prosecutor's office, seized 39.8 kilograms of cocaine hydrochloride on board the coal transport vessel named "Ping May" and of the flag of Liberia.
La operacin fue desarrollada gracias al fortalecimiento de las unidades y de los controles portuarios por parte de la Armada Nacional, que en este caso especfico permitieron adelantar investigaciones en coordinacin con el CTI de la Fiscala, logrando establecer que el buque de carga estaba contaminado. The operation was developed thanks to the strengthening of the units and the port controls by the National Navy, which in this specific case allowed to carry out investigations in coordination with the CTI of the Prosecutor's Office, establishing that the cargo ship was contaminated.
La inspeccin a la embarcacin, que se encontraba fondeada en el sector de Cinaga - Magdalena, fue realizada por parte de unidades de la Estacin de Guardacostas de Santa Marta y personal del CTI, quienes hallaron a bordo dos bultos con 40 paquetes que contenan el alcaloide, que estaban ubicados en el paol de cadenas. The inspection of the vessel, which was anchored in the Cinaga - Magdalena sector, was carried out by units of the Coast Guard Station of Santa Marta and CTI personnel, who found on board two packages with 40 packages containing the alkaloid. , which were located in the chain store. Personal del CTI seccional de Magdalena efectu las pruebas respectivas dando positivo para 39.8 kilogramos de clorhidrato de cocana. Personnel of the ITC sectional of Magdalena carried out the respective tests giving positive for 39.8 kilograms of cocaine hydrochloride. No hubo personas capturadas. There were no people captured.
El buque carbonero Ping May arrib a Colombia hace seis das, procedente de Inglaterra y tena como rumbo el puerto de Rotterdam - Holanda. The "Ping May" coal ship arrived in Colombia six days ago, coming from England and heading to the port of Rotterdam - Holland. Este es el quinto buque extranjero de carga que se ha confirmado que ha sido contaminado con alcaloides en el puerto de Santa Marta. This is the fifth foreign cargo ship that has been confirmed to have been contaminated with alkaloids in the port of Santa Marta.
En lo corrido del ao, ya son ms de 28 toneladas de cocana las incautadas por la Armada Nacional en el Caribe colombiano. So far this year, there are already more than 28 tons of cocaine seized by the National Navy in the Colombian Caribbean.
La Armada Nacional continuar adelantando operaciones con el fin de garantizar el uso legal del mar y combatir el narcotrfico y sus diferentes modalidades empleadas para el trfico de estupefacientes. The National Navy will continue advancing operations in order to guarantee the legal use of the sea and combat drug trafficking and its different methods used for drug trafficking.
