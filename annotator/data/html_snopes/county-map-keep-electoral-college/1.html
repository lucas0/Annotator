2018 National and State Population Estimates

December 19, 2018

News Release

PRESS RELEASE | DECEMBER 19, 2018

Nevada and Idaho Are the Nation’s Fastest-Growing States

The U.S. population grew by 0.6 percent and Nevada and Idaho were the nation’s fastest-growing states between July 1, 2017, and July 1, 2018.

STORY | DECEMBER 20, 2018

States in South and West Growing the Fastest

The U.S. population grew by 0.6 percent between July 1, 2017, and July 1, 2018 and the five fastest-growing states were all in the South and West.
