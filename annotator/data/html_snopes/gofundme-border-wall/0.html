Rusty Humphries
Reporter and Host, "Rusty Humphries Rebellion"

Rusty Humphries is an, award winning, veteran of the radio and TV industry. He has been a successful political consultant, appeared in five major motion pictures. The Rusty Humphries Rebellion,” is one of the world’s most popular podcasts on politics and pop-culture, often ranking in the top 50 podcasts.

A dynamic speaker and personality, he’s been seen on nearly all national broadcast and cable outlets including Fox News Channel, NBC, ABC, CBS, CNN, MSNBC, RT, History, NatGeo, Amazon and many others.

His exploits have been fodder for books, TV and radio shows, and have even been fictionalized by multi-award winning, bestselling author Orson Scott Card in the book “Hidden Empire.”

Humphries is an expert in broadcasting and social media training as well as business and talent development.  He is the CEO of a thriving consulting agency.

Don Irvine
Reporter and Host, "Accuracy in America with Don Irvine"

Don Irvine is the chairman of Accuracy in Media and its sister organization Accuracy in Academia. As the son of Reed Irvine, who launched AIM in 1969, he developed an understanding of media bias at an early age, and has been actively involved with AIM for over 30 years.

Besides directing the day-to-day operations of AIM and AIA, Don has launched a number of other projects, including the American Journalism Center (AJC), an internship program for conservative student journalists founded in 2005, and the AIM Center for Investigative Journalism, launched in 2010 to provide another outlet for objective, fact-based news coverage.

Don travels extensively to attend and speak at political events, and has appeared on numerous radio and TV shows. He produces video profiles of leading conservative legislators, and has become one of the conservative movement’s leading coalition builders and promoters of new media technology. Don is active on Facebook and Twitter. You can follow him @donirvine to read his latest thoughts.

Mason Weaver
Reporter and Host "Stay Right With Mason Weaver"

Mason has been the national and regional spokesperson for many groups and organizations. A 1975 Graduate of UC Berkeley Political Science Department. He served as the Second Vice Chair of the San Diego Republican Party in 1999. Stood as a candidate for the California State Assembly 2000 and the US Congress 2010.

He is a noted conference speaker and guest lecture. Mason Weaver has appeared on national TV, cable and news programs.

Mason hosted a live call in radio program and wrote a weekly column on social and political issues. He soon became a sought after public speaker and lecturer. Mason is gifted with an extraordinary view of government and business.

Mason Weaver is the author of It’s OK to Leave the Plantation, The Rope, Diamond in the Rough, and Polishing the Diamond in the Rough which discusses the social issues that affect us all and that bind us together. Noted for his quick wit, vigorous debate and forceful oratory, Mason is a challenge to the opposition and an instructive persuasive counselor to allies.
