Dead Heat by Joel C. Rosenberg Now Available
Dead Heat is the fifth and final installment in Rosenberg’s series that includes The Last Jihad, The Last Days, The Ezekiel Option, and The Copper Scroll. Known for writing stories that seem “ripped from tomorrow’s headlines,” Rosenberg has created political thrillers full of scenarios that come true in part.

In Dead Heat he portrays a nation so distracted by a hotly-contested presidential race that it is blindsided by a terrorist attack. “I pray to God this novel never comes true,” says author Joel C. Rosenberg. “To misunderstand the nature and threat of evil is to risk being blindsided. Since September 11, 2001, the U.S. has been extraordinarily successful in disrupting terrorist plots against the American homeland. But we dare not be lulled into a false sense of complacency. There are nations and groups out there planning our destruction. Are we ready? Will our next president be ready?”
