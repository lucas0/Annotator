Info
A Zimbabwean media organisation focused on encouraging & participating in progressive national dialogue
What began as a twitter account and hashtag in September 2012, 263Chat has grown to become an online publication focused on creating, encouraging and convening ...national and progressive dialogue focused on Zimbabwe. 263Chat publishes the news via its website: www.263chat.com and uses social media tools to disseminate its content. The use of video content is critical in sharing the Zimbabwean narrative with its wider audience.
