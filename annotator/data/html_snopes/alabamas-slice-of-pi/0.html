Purdue tradition provides AGEC student with a lifetime of memories

Annalee Witte (Junior; Sales and Marketing; Wilkinson, IN) served as a host during the 2018 Purdue's Old Masters Program. The Program, which dates back to 1950, brings back ten Purdue University alumni who have made a significant contribution to their field with the hopes that they will share their ideas and experiences with the student body. Each alumnus is paired with five current Purdue students who act as liasons or host/hostess for their Old Master. These students accompanying the Old Masters to various classroom talks, lunch engagements, and evening banquets. Read More
