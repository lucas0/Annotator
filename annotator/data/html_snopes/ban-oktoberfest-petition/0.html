Wawa bring back the hot roast beef sandwiches

This petition had 68 supporters

Lou Kleinworth started this petition to wawa
The hot roast beef was & is the best sandwich Wawa ever sold. Wawa discontinued it and substituted it with prime rib, which was really good as well. Then they discontinued prime rib and brought pulled pork to the menu, which I don't even think is available any more. The fact of the matter is, I wish to see the hot roast beef back, for good. I am not the only 1 who feels this way. There is numerous people that agree. If you are 1 of them, please sign this petition so we can show Wawa how missed the hot roast beef sandwich is.
