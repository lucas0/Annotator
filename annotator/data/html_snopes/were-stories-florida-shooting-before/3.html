Wrong date showing up on search results

We have a strange issue we've noticed where the date beside articles shows incorrectly in Google search. We are also indexed by Google News and the date is correct, but for whatever reason it does not show correctly in search.

Example:http://www.google.com/search?sclient=psy&hl=en&source=hp&q=%22What+to+do+in+Central+London+in+late+August%22&pbx=1&oq=%22What+to+do+in+Central+London+in+late+August%22&aq=f&aqi=&gs_upl=1955l1955l3l2262l1l1l1l0l0l0l0l0ll0l0&bav=on.2,or.r_gc.r_pw.&biw=1440&bih=815

We're looking at the results for this article on our site:http://www.digitaljournal.com/article/310404

The article was published August 16, 2011 and the time stamp is correct on the Google News results. But take note of the date stamp on the search results -- it says it was published August 1, 2011.

Can anyone help us understand why this is happening and what we can do to fix it? Your help is very much appreciated!
