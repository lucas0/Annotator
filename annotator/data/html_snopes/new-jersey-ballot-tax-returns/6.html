SB94 SD1       (?)


Measure Title: RELATING TO ELECTIONS.
Report Title: Presidential Ballots; General Election; Income Tax Return
Description: Requires candidates for president and vice president of the United States to post their most recent income tax return on the Internet at least sixty days prior to and continuing through the date of the general election for free access by the public in order to be included on the state ballot, subject to certain conditions. Prohibits electors from voting for a candidate for president or vice president of the United States unless the candidates have posted their most recent income tax return on the Internet at least sixty days prior to and continuing through the date of the general election for free access by the public, subject to certain conditions. (SD1)
Companion:  HB286
Package: None
Current Referral: JUD
Introducer(s): K. RHOADS, HARIMOTO, IHARA, KEITH-AGARAN
