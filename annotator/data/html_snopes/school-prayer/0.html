Welcome

The students and staff of Roane County High School welcome you to our web site. Look for regular changes to this site as we develop it to better serve the students, staff, and visitors to this site. This site will always be under construction as changes and improvements are made.

 

Our network is connected to the Internet via a T1 leased line to our district's central office and then the Tennessee Educational Network. Our web site is served by a Macintosh G3 Server running AppleShare IP version 6.
