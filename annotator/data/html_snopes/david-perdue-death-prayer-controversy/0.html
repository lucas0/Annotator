The Faith and Freedom Coalition “Road to Majority” conference is the premier national event for people of faith and conservative activists. We expect over 2,000 grassroots activists from across the country to gather in Washington, DC for Road To Majority 2019. Attendees will leave Road To Majority better prepared and energized to mobilize voters of faith to bring about a pro-family majority in Washington and in their states through training for voter registration and voter education campaigns, get-out-the-vote, lobbying, media relations, building a precinct organization, and utilizing social media.

President Donald J. Trump To Headline Faith & Freedom Coalition 2019 Road To Majority Policy Conference 

Washington, DC – President Donald J. Trump will address the Faith & Freedom Coalition Road To Majority Policy Conference for the sixth time and his second appearance as President of the United States, on Wednesday, June 26 at the Marriott Wardman Park Hotel in Washington, DC. 

“We are pleased and honored to welcome President Trump back to the Road to Majority Policy Conference stage. On the key issue priorities of the faith community, President Trump has delivered on his promises, including the confirmation of two Supreme Court Justices and over 100 lower court judges, protecting the sanctity of unborn life, defending religious freedom, passing historic criminal justice reform and supporting the state of Israel,” said Faith & Freedom Chairman Ralph Reed. “He will receive an enthusiastic welcome from thousands of Faith & Freedom activists.”

“Our members are excited to welcome President Trump to Road to Majority again,” said Tim Head, executive director of Faith & Freedom Coalition. “This will be our largest Policy Conference ever by attendance and our activists are energized and enthusiastic.”

Road to Majority, the premier national event for people of faith, will take place Wednesday, June 26 through Saturday, June 29 in Washington, DC. President Trump will kick-off the four-day conference next Wednesday at 10:30 am.

Chairman Ralph Reed On The Ingraham Angle To Discuss President Trump’s Speech To Faith & Freedom Coalition

Washington, DC – Faith & Freedom Coalition Chairman Ralph Reed appeared on last night’s edition of the Ingraham Angle on Fox News to discuss President Donald Trump’s speech before a packed house at the Road To Majority Policy Conference in Washington, DC.

A History of Success

Faith and Freedom's 2018 "Road to Majority" Conference a Record Shattering Success!

The 2018 Faith & Freedom Coalition “ Road to Majority ” conference was the largest and most successful to date.

The media and the radical Left are determined to stop the Trump legislative agenda, including the repeal of ObamaCare, ending taxpayer funding of abortion, and the confirmation of conservative judges to the federal bench. We must take this golden opportunity to advance our shared pro-family values in Congress, state legislatures, and local government.

Road to Majority ” featured an array of speakers, unprecedented in modern American political history, including: Vice President Mike Pence, Speaker of the House Paul Ryan, Senate Majority Leader Mitch McConnell, House Majority Leader Kevin McCarthy, Israeli Ambassador Ron Dermer, Kellyanne Conway, Charles Krauthammer, Eric Metaxas, and many others.

We hope that you take the encouragement and knowledge gained through three days of speeches, panel discussions, and praise and worship back to your community and continue the fight for our shared values, including religious liberty and the sanctity of human life.
