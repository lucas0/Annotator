About

Houston Leader is Houston’s most trusted source for local news. With a focus on local content, the Leader thrives to maintain a non-partisan newsroom making our content the most reliable source available in print and across the web. With an investigative team of 12 reporters and editors with a combined 195 years of experience we bring you the local news that matters.
