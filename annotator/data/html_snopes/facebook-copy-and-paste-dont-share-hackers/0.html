Joe Barton-Holme The Oxford Dictionary definition of hacking is to "gain unauthorised access to data in a system or computer". So if I gain unauthorised access to people's Facebook accounts by installing a keylogger on a computer in an internet café, and in doing so capture their Facebook login credentials, then I have hacked their Facebook accounts. If I hack Yahoo and steal 500 million user accounts and then try and succeed in logging into a number Facebook profiles using the same email address and password combinations then I have gained unauthorised access to those accounts and therefore hacked them. People's Facebook accounts are being hacked all the time; rogue apps are one of many, many routes and by no means is it the most common.
