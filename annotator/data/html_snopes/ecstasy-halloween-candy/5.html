User Report: I had heard a lot of good things about these pills so a couple of weeks ago I managed to get hold of some. Took them in a night club, I was pretty drunk when consumed so my timing might not have been that accurate

+0:00h - bit off the body, leaving the head. So took maybe 2/3 of the pill.

+0:45h - Started getting butterflies in my stomach, but in a good way, can really feel it to start taking effect

+1h - Absolutely pinging now, no head fuzzyness just pure rush. Music is sounding fucking intense and getting a bit of jaw clenching

+1:30h - Initial rush has chilled out a bit but feeling really good, only way i can describe it is pinging. My mate took a whole one and is mashed, definitely glad I took half

+2h - Dropped the last of it (the head), can feel effects starting to fade a little so probably about time to finish

+2:30h - Feeling really buzzing again, definitely the best pills i've had in the past few months

+4h - Starting to come off it a bit now, couldnt manage one sip of a drink all night because i was so buzzing! no mongy feeling as well, which I can't stand from some pills.

+5:30h - On the way home now, feeling the standard after effects of dropping. Looking forward to a good sleep.


Next day - Didnt feel much of a comedown, and to be honest i've been taking pills for a long time now and don't really seem to suffer from that effect anymore. Just a bit of a hang over from the predrinks!

All in all, very good pills. Would DEFINITELY recommend biting a bit off, either take the head if you're inexperienced, or if you've taken mdma before, then the body.
