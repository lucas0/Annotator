Prepare for Wildfire Season 

Be ready for wildfire before it strikes by visiting CalFire's "Ready for Wildfire" web page.

CalFire's "Ready, Set, Go!" campaign provides practical advance preparation steps for home protection and rapid evacuation.

Download CalFire's "Go!" Wildfire Evacuation Guide here.

CodeRED Alert System

During local emergencies such as the current Klamathon Fire, immediate notifications and updates are crucial. All residents and businesses are encouraged to sign up for the CodeRED emergency alert system. To receive emergency information, click on the CodeRED icon below. If you need administrative assistance signing up, contact the Yreka Police Department.

Yreka Utility Customer Warning:

There is an on-line bill pay website that appears to be affiliated with the City, but it is not. Doxo.com is a third-party marketing website that allows people to pay multiple company utility bills via their site.

 

To make sure you are paying the City directly, use the "Pay Your Utility Bill" button to the right to reach our online bill payment portal at:

https://yrekacityutilitybill.merchanttransact.com

For more information, please call 530-841-2386.

Drought Information

Due to the good precipitation over the winter Governor Brown has ended the drought State of Emergency in most of California. The City is now able to ease water restrictions. Voluntary conservation measures are in place, along with State mandated prohibitions that discourage water waste. Follow the link below to learn more:

View City of Yreka Utilities "Water Efficiency" page.
