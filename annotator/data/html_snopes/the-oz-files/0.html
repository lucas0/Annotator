Oz Books by L. Frank Baum

There are fourteen books in the Wizard of Oz series by Baum, plus a book of short stories. The following lists these by date of first publication. To see a short summary of the adventures related in a book, click on it.
  • 1900 The Wonderful Wizard of Oz
  • 1904 The Marvelous Land of Oz
  • 1907 Ozma of Oz
  • 1908 Dorothy and the Wizard in Oz
  • 1909 The Road to Oz
  • 1910 The Emerald City of Oz
  • 1913 The Patchwork Girl of Oz
  • 1914 Little Wizard Stories of Oz
  • 1914 Tik-Tok of Oz
  • 1915 The Scarecrow of Oz
  • 1916 Rinkitink in Oz
  • 1917 The Lost Princess of Oz
  • 1918 The Tin Woodman of Oz
  • 1919 The Magic of Oz
  • 1920 Glinda of Oz
