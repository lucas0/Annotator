President Dr Mokgweetsi Masisi has extended good wishes to Members of Parliament and their families for the forthcoming festive season and new year during a luncheon he hosted for legislators and their spouses at the State House on December 10. At the beginning of a week where the legislators are due to wind up the current parliamentary session, MPs shared lunch with the president and First Lady Neo Masisi, both of whom expressed good wishes t o the legislators ahead of the Christmas and New Year period.
