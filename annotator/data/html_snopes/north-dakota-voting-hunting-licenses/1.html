ID Card Requirements

Requirements

An ND Drivers License Site must be visited to obtain a North Dakota non-driver photo identification card.

What is an ID Card

You may obtain an ID card at any age. If you are at least 14 years of age and wish to be identified as a donor on your identification card, you may do so by signing the donor identification block on the application form.

Identification Requirements

  • Proof of Identification is Required

Residency

Any person who has lived in this state 90 consecutive days shall be deemed a resident of North Dakota for the purpose of non-driver photo identification card issuance. Proof of North Dakota resident address is required. You must be a resident of North Dakota to obtain a non-driver photo identification card.

  • Acceptable Proof of Residential Address
