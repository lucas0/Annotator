    Susana González (II)

    Actress
    Susana González was born on October 2, 1973 in Calera, Zacatecas, Mexico as Susana Alejandra González Del Río. She is an actress, known for Sexo y otros secretos (2007), Heridas de amor (2006) and Gotita de amor (1998). See full bio »
