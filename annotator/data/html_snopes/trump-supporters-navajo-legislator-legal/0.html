Details
Living United for Change in Arizona (LUCHA) is an organization trying to advance social, racial and economic justice for DACA recipients, illegal aliens and their illegal families. They are social justice warriors promoting illegal immigration and open borders throughout Arizona.

Join us as we infiltrate and protest LUCHA at the Arizona State Capitol. We will attend their event, ask questions and let the voices of Americans be heard. America First! No more illegals!

LUCHA plans to meet with our state legislators and present their "2018 LUCHA Platform".

According to their event, both Representatives and Senators will be invited to sign on to the "LUCHA platform" as they remind them that our communities are watching closely and they will hold them accountable.

Here is a blurb from their website. http://www.luchaaz.org/

“They can take away DACA but they will never take away our power” - Abril Gallardo
DACA was a huge step forward - but it doesn’t define us. Nobody can take away our humanity or dignity - nor can they grant it with the stroke of a pen. Immigrants were here before DACA and we will be here long after. We are resilient, we are guerreros y guerreras, and we are #HereToStay!

Attorney General Jeff Sessions, on behalf of the entire Trump administration, just announced that he will end DACA by March 5th, 2018. This decision will affect nearly 1 million young immigrants. This government seeks to divide us by trying to negotiate our lives, our families and all 11 million undocumented hermanos y hermanas in this country - but my family or my community are not for trade!

Please sign this petition to stand with DACA beneficiaries and the undocumented community, and let's show that we are not backing down and that we will protect immigrants from Trump’s racist attacks.
