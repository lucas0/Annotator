Dalton is a great community in which to live, work and play! Our mission is to provide a prosperous and safe environment with effective, professional municipal services that promote business and industry; a high quality of life for residents; and positive interaction between all community members. In order to achieve this mission, we rely on the cooperation and participation of all community members. We believe that the purpose of local government is to, first and foremost, provide service.
