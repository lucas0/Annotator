1.Mix cornstarch and water and it becomes a non-newtonian fluid. That means you can walk on it.

2.The number of possible ways to arrange a 52 card deck is about the number of atoms in the galaxy. Because: 52! = 8.06581752 × 10^67

3.You can fart in space, but it’s not recommended.

4.The bushes and clouds in Super Mario Bros are the same, just colored differently.

5. There are warehouses so big they have their own WEATHER.

The Nasa Vehicle Assembly Building has its own rainclouds.

6.Blue whales heart is the size of a VW Beetle and that you could swim through some of its arteries.

7.There are more atoms in a single glass of water, than glasses of water in all the oceans of the Earth.

8.Alcohol is considered a performance-enhancing drug in competitive shooting competitions.

9.Once Charlie Chaplin entered a contest for “Charlie Chaplin look-alikes” and he came in third.

10.Pirates wear eye patches so that they can see in the dark.When they go to a lower deck with poor lighting, they can remove the patch and see better with that eye since it’s used to the dark, whereas the other eye would take several minutes to adjust to the change in light.

11.You replace every particle in your body every seven years. You are literally not the same person you were 7 years ago.

12.A tree is the opposite of your lungs. Physically and functionally.

13.Goats have rectangular pupils.

14.If there was no space between any of its atoms, Earth would be the size of a baseball.

15.Sloths mistakenly grab their arms instead of the branches of a tree, and they fall to their deaths.

16.Penguins will give their mate a pebble as a way of proposing.

17.Turtles can breathe out of their butts.

18.Lighters were invented before matches.

19.1 sperm has 37.5MB of DNA information in it. That means a normal ejaculation represents a data transfer of 1587GB in about 3 seconds

20.A Mosquito has 47 teeth.

21.A banana is actually a berry. A strawberry isn’t.
