CAUGHT ON CAMERA!! Angel On White Horse Fighting In Israel

By FrancisMary | August 22, 2017

Giant Angels riding white horses might seem a little far fetched to some but it’s hard to deny that there is definitely something amazing going on in this short video below…

One can see 14 seconds into the video it appears an angelic being riding a horse INTERCEPTS and hits a fighter jet and moments later, sure enough, the jet crashes into a field. Whatever this truly might be, it’s certainly got all the people on the ground cheering.

In the bible Revelation 19:11 actually speaks about this white horse “And I saw heaven opened, and behold a white horse, and he that sat upon him was called Faithful and True, and in righteousness, he doth judge and make war.”

It’s so easy to see the comparison here in that verse. Perhaps the Angel decided to take to these skies the protector of this battle? Since Angels are made by God – this shows us that God is still on the throne keeping watch – and He cares enough to show us GREAT wonders such as in this video.
