New - Dragon Soop Stingray

Dragon Soop - A fermented alcoholic beverage fortified with Schnapps 

Dragon Soop is 8% abv and contains high levels of caffeine (35mg per 100ml), blended with taurine, guarana and delicious flavours.

Dragon Soop comes in nine flavours – Dark Fruit Punch, Blue Raspberry, Passion Fruit & Orange, Red Kola, Sour Apple, Strawberry & Lime, Tropical Fruit Punch, Venom and now NEW Stingray!

Always drink in moderation and remember it is not suitable for persons sensitive to caffeine.

 

Dragon Soop is an 8% abv fermented alcoholic beverage fortified with Schnapps, containing high levels of caffeine (35mg per 100ml), blended with taurine, guarana and delicious flavours, in a chillable can. The number of units of alcohol per can (4 units in a 500ml can) is clearly marked, so you can drink aware.

 

Dragon Soop is one of the first enhanced alcoholic beverages in a can to be available through UK off licences. So now you can take it home. Chill. And enjoy!
