Due to a couple instances of illness in dogs given the product, we received a number of calls today regarding rawhide "candy canes" purchased at Walmart. We are... not aware of any official recalls of the product and the incidents are few enough, to our knowledge, that there is no solid proof that those particular rawhides caused the symptoms. We do, however, encourage dog owners to be judicious in their choice of treats. Please choose treats produced with good quality control (e.g. made in the USA); choose rawhide-type products that are easily digestible; give treats in proportion to animal size; ensure that your pet is not swallowing large pieces of the treat; and consider that animals with sensitive intestinal tracts may not tolerate certain treats. The following article provides a fairly balanced view of the subject.
