WALMART CHICKEN WINGS MADE FROM ‘RAT MEAT’ SAYS FDA

.
Posted By: Now44News
We have learned that United States Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is now very concerned that an estimated one million pounds of rat meat is being passed off as ‘chicken wings’ been sold in Walmart stores across America.
According to reports, FDA inspectors raised concerns after several illegal shipping containers originating from China were seized by customs at the Port of San Francisco.
It was found to contain rat meat that was meant to be shipped to different meat processing plants across America and resold as chicken wings in Walmart.
“Customers could have been eating rat meat for years as its near impossible to tell the difference between rat and chicken meat as they taste the same” – FDA
