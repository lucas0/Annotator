this is bizarre: think twice about drinking Aquafina
following ken2's advice about icing down fish, i bought a case of aquafina bottled water and put it my bait freezer. after four days all but 5 were frozen solid.......worked great. come back from fishing a couple of days later and the five in the freezer have still not frozen AT ALL.....zero. put the others back in to refreeze and they have since refrozen in 3 days. it has been 10 days now and the 5 bottles that did not freeze before have still not frozen......zero. when you take them out of the freezer liquid inside must be below 32 degrees because the condensation on the outside of the plastic freezes instantly.......sort of like a bottle your favorite tequila in the freezer.

this is really weird.....these are unopened bottles of aquafina. makes you wonder what exactly is in those bottles that won't freeze. they have been all over the inside of the freezer in and amongst the other bottles. the frozen ones are easy to spot because when they freeze they expand slightly and the label comes right off.

so, think twice about guzzling down that ice cold bottle of aquafina........what exactly is in it?
