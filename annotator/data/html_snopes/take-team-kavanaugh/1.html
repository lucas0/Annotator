Opportunities in Culinary Industry

Chef Patty Labeau teaches the Fundamental Chef classes in the Career and Technical Industry classes for high school students at the DCTC campus. In a real-world commercial kitchen students prepare meals, learn about food safety, and practice a variety of culinary techniques. The program is open to 11th and 12th grade students from any of the nine member districts, or attend the ISD 917 DCALS program. Some of the photos above feature the dessert bar that was open to the public. The desserts tasted even better than they looked. Come and join us to prepare for a career in the food industry.


The students in Chef Cherry Cramer's culinary classes have a terrific opportunity to learn hands-on skills that will serve them well as they prepare for careers in in the food service industry. Each spring the Cherry Nook Sidewalk Cafe makes an appearance as a "pop-up" cafe serving a delicious assortment of lunch items. Students host, cook, serve, clean, and cashier.
