After Grotesque Trump Audio Revealed, Melania Announces She’s Backing Hillary

Written by: S. Wood
0
It looks as though Donald Trump’s wife Melania has finally had enough of his shenanigans. No, she’s not divorcing him, but she’s also not backing him for president anymore after what was just revealed.
Audio was released showing Donald Trump speaking very lewdly to Access Hollywood‘s Billy Bush back in 2005. The audio was recorded right after his marriage to Melania, and revealed him talking about him kissing other women, claiming he could get any woman he wanted because he’s rich and famous, as well as boldly saying he can even go up and grab a woman’s p***y.
Yes, that’s the word you think it is.
Well, not being able to put up with yet another horrific display by her husband, whom she has defended time and time again, she finally made the announcement that she’s “with her.”
In a phone call to Free Wood Post, Melania said:
“I’ve come to you because of your accuracy in reporting. You’ll tell the whole truth and nothing but the truth. It’s as simple as this. After listening to my husband making those comments about sexually assaulting other women without their permission, I simply cannot stand by and support him for president any longer. I may still have to put up with him, but America shouldn’t have to. That is why I’m announcing my endorsement of Hillary Clinton. Yes, it’s true. I’m with her.”
Honestly, we can’t say we’re surprised. This was a long time coming. Congratulations to Melania for making a wise decision. Hopefully, the rest of America will also notice what an absolutely disgusting asshole he is and vote against him this November.
