BREAKING NEWS: Adelaide club shut down for killing patrons and selling as meat

By
Country Correspondent

The Australian Federal Police has shut down a nightclub, Oasis Nightclub, in the city of Adelaide after investigation uncovered that the owner killed patrons of the facility and served to others as meat.
The Police said in a statement that they acted swiftly upon tip-off and a search at the club revealed human parts kept in deep freezers.
The Oasis Nightclub, foreign-owned, started operating in February 2019.
The statement added that the owner of the facility has been arrested and other three people believed to be accomplices are at large.
