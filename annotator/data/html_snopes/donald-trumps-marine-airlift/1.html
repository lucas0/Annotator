ABSTRACT LT GEN VERNON J. KONDRA SERVED AS DEPUTY CHIEF OF STAFF FOR OPERATIONS AT HEADQUARTERS MILITARY AIRLIFT COMMAND (MAC) DURING OPERATION DESERT SHIELD/DESERT STORM. LT GEN KONDRA EXPANDS AND EXPLAINS NOTES THAT HE MADE FROM 24 AUG 1990 THROUGH 31 MAY 1991 CONCERNING THE PERSIAN GULF WAR. MENTIONS MATCHING LOADS WITH AIRCRAFT INTO SAUDI ARABIA FROM THE UNITED STATES; REFUELING PROBLEMS ON THE GROUND AT DHAHRAN, SAUDI ARABIA; AIRCREW MANAGEMENT PROBLEMS; USE OF TIGER TEAMS; USE OF KUWAITI 747 AIRCRAFT; AIRBORNE REFUELING; DEPLOYMENT OF 94 AND 130 PROVISIONAL TACTICAL AIRLIFT SQUADRON (PTAS). REMEMBERS C-5 AIRCRAFT CRASH AT RAMSTEIN, GERMANY ON 29 AUG 1990. DEER STRUCK ON RUNWAYS AT RAMSTEIN ON 1 SEP 1990. MEDICAL TEAM ESTABLISHED TO CONDUCT AIRCREW STRESS TEST; RECOMMEND GOOD NUTRITION AND REST FOR CREWS. DISCUSSES POLICY FOR CALLING UP MILITARY RESERVISTS AND PLAN FOR RESERVE ROTATION. DESERT EXPRESS, DAILY FLIGHT FROM UNITED STATES TO RIYADH, SAUDI ARABIA, STARTED ON 12 OCT 1990. ALL MAIL SENT BY AIR. QUESTIONS CONCERNING AIR EVACUATION CAPABILITIES. CONTRACTED TRUMP SHUTTLE TO MOVE TROOPS IN CONTINENTAL UNITED STATES (CONUS). JAPAN PROVIDED $36 MILLION TO BUY NEW PALLETS AND NETS. DESCRIBES SOMBER MOOD AT MAC COMMAND POST AS WAR STARTED ON 17 JAN 1991. CONCERN OVER PALLETS, NETS, AND STRAPS; ESTABLISHED A PALLET RECOVERY TEAM. SCHEDULING AIRCRAFT MISSIONS AROUND SCUD ATTACKS. CRITERIA FOR AERIAL ACHIEVEMENT MEDAL (AAM). ISRAELI AIRLIFT SUPPORTED MOVE OF PATRIOT MISSILES. REDEPLOYMENT AIRLIFTS FOR TROOPS CARRIED 3,000 PASSENGERS PER DAY. LAST FLIGHT OF DESERT EXPRESS ON 18 MAY 1991. MENTIONS COMMUNICATION AND PATIENCE AS REQUIREMENT FOR LEADERSHIP.
