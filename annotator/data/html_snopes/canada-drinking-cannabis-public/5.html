You Can Be Fined $1500 For Drinking Or Smoking Weed In Public Starting Next Month

admin 2 days ago News Comments Off on You Can Be Fined $1500 For Drinking Or Smoking Weed In Public Starting Next Month 1,450 Views
Mayors are considering to bring this new tougher laws in every province of Canada, you will no longer will be able to consume alcohol or smoke weed responsibly in parks or on beaches.
Fines vary from province to province, but they can be up to $1000 in in provinces like British Columbia and Ontario, Quebec, Alberta, Nova Scotia, Manitoba, Newfoundland and Labrador.
If you get caught with open alcohol in Toronto right now, you could face a fine of up to $125 and a whopping $230 in Vancouver.
Tougher rules are coming for most people who like to consume alcohol or weed in public.
Starting next month if  you are caught drinking beer or smook weed in public , the police will pour out your alcohol and make you pay a fine $1500.
