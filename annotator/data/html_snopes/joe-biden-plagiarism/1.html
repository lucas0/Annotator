    Biden Withdrawal

    1987-09-24T01:19:30-04:00https://images.c-span.org/Files/5fe/000820-m.jpgSenator Biden announced his withdrawal from the Democratic presidential primary race, citing reasons of certain mistakes he made, plus his responsibility to the Senate Judiciary Committee. The committee was conducting hearings on the Judge Robert Bork nomination to the U.S. Supreme Court at the time. Senator Biden did not stay for questions, but his advisers Mr. Martilla and Ms. Biden-Owens answered reporters' questions.
