Make America Great Again (MAGA)! Remember, that is not just a p...olitical statement, but a "life statement". This club is organized to be a clearinghouse for all great ideas that can make us individually AND as a group, great again. President Trump's agenda is not purely political, but a mission statement for all of us to improve ourselves AND our country, so we can help make the world a better place for everyone. Feel free to share all the positive ways we can make that happen. When we are stronger, we are able to help others. Don't forget to add other Trump supporters as members as this will help us be a viable force in the elections as well as achieving the Trump Agenda.

All members that are invited or accepted are allowed to post comments that are positive to our mission of supporting the Trump agenda and Making America Great Again. You are also welcome to add other members, no matter where they are in the country. We can try to connect them to local groups in their area and also help them set up their own groups! Our volunteers were on the phones and social media ALL over the country in the last election, even helping in other states during the primaries and getting out the vote!
