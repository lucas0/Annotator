 *1.1 [--] Trackdown: THE MARPLE BROTHERS
04-Oct-1957 CBS Fri  (Rerun 20Jun58)
Directed by Thomas Carr
Written By John McGreevey
Starring: Robert Culp (as Hoby Gilman)
Guest Cast:
James Best.........as Rand Marple
James Griffith.........as Thad Marple (recurring)
Jan Merlin.........as Cece Marnie
Gail Kobe.........as Beth (later recurring as Penny Adams)
Tom Pittman.........as Tom Sladen
Roy Engel.........as The Marshall
Don House.........as The Doctor
Sheb Wooley.........as The Sharpshooter
Synopsis:
Ranger Hoby Gillman is on the trail of the notorious Marple Brothers and their
gang wanted for murder and robbery. He tracks them to the town of Stockton,
Texas and finds that the outlaws are holding woman and children hostages in the local church.
He must take the Marple Brothers without endangering the townspeople's lives. [SC]

1.03 [--] Trackdown: SAN SABA INCIDENT
18-Oct-1957 CBS Fri
Directed By Donald McDougall
Written By D.D.Beachamp [SC]
Starring: Robert Culp (as Hoby Gilman)
Guest Cast: [SC]
Rex Reason.........as Travis, The Stranger
Margaret Hayes.........as Abby Lindon
Mort Mills.........as Ike Collins
Guy Wilkerson.........as Dutchy Hoffman
Chris Alcaide.........as Prisoner#1
Robert Karnes.........as Prisoner#2.
[Note: The storyline for this episode was re-used again for a "Wanted Dead
Or Alive" episode titled 'Prison Trail'(1960) It was scripted by the same
writer D.D.Beauchamp who only changed the charactor names.]
Synopsis:
Texas Ranger Hoby Gilman sets out for the state penitentiary at Sat Saba with a
wagonload of prisoners, one of them a beautiful woman who has been convicted
of manslaughter. The Ranger suddenly finds that the wagon is being followed,
and he prepares to defend his captives.

1.06 [--] Trackdown: SWEETWATER, TEXAS
08-Nov-1957 CBS Fri

Synopsis 1:
Hoby Gillman comes upon the remains of a stagecoach hold-up. The only survivor
is a baby. Gillman finds a note with the name of a woman and a town called
Sweetwater. After leaving the infant with a local doctor,Hoby investigates
and his only suspects to the murder and robbery are a gambler and a drummer.
[SC]
Synopsis 2:
Gilman comes upon a stagecoach that has been robbed, with all the passengers
dead except for a baby. His only clue is the phonograph of a woman which he finds on the scene.
