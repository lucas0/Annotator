House -Spoiler Infos about the Season Finale

May 4
Posted by housemdaddict
.
hypable.com
Exclusive ‘House’ finale spoilers, on set photos gathered by fans
New set photos and spoilers direct from the set of House have made their way online courtesy of a series fan. They’ve been shared with us so you can enjoy!
In these first two photos, they are setting up a shot with Lisa Edelstein, Hugh Laurie and Robert Sean Leonard outside “Cuddy’s House.” It was a scene with the three leads arguing and Cuddy in tears.
This next photo is the back of “House’s” car. There were a couple on the street that day, one with the window shield missing. I’m guessing they removed it for interior shots. They shot House and Wilson driving up and down the street, arguing.

In the final photo, Princeton police arrive at Cuddy’s house. There’s rumor of a car accident and even of the car driving into her house. They did shot a big car scene on a closed stage so that might have been the car crash.

While our source’s friend was there, she heard the director, Greg Yaitanes, say they were filming the opening teaser for the finale, but this is not confirmed. Yaitanes notorious for messing around with fans.
If it’s true, the finale will be like last season’s with flash backs and a very surprising ending.


Latest ‘House’ on-set photos show nighttime shooting at Cuddy’s house for finale
We’ve received more photos and a report from the set ofHouse, where filming for the season finale was recently underway!

These scenes were filmed in a different location behind Cuddy’s house. Filming was split between Cuddy’s house and this spot. My source has no clue what is going on (and really, because it isHouse, it can be anything) but the scene and filming looked intense.
According to my source, scenes involving House and Wilson arguing in the car were filmed during the day. Then House gets out of the car and goes into Cuddy’s house alone. Filming continued inside so of course my source couldn’t see much.
Once House and Wilson are in the front yard again, now in the evening, they continue arguing until they react as if an explosion happened, “jumping back and covering their eyes.”
In the photo below you can see the police squad cars and fire department present at the scene. Cuddy was reportedly speaking with a paramedic or police officer out of this frame.

In the next photo the ambulance and police cars were filming in front of an unknown house. Wilson is in the ambulance! It looked like he wasn’t hurt badly but still was reported to have a bandage on his arm.
A Princeton PD cruiser sits in front of a neighbor’s house in this last photo. With the new promo photos out, this finale looks more interesting by the minute!
