About WIN

The Wireless Industry Network (WIN) program is a coast-to-coast grassroots program designed to promote NATE’s regional, state and local efforts, support existing state and national wireless organizations and facilitate communication between all stakeholders in the wireless industry.

WIN consists of a nationwide network of Regional Ambassadors, State and Provincial Liaisons who are devoted to NATE’s mission and are passionate about expanding the Association’s message of safety, quality, standards and education in order to reach all stakeholders in the wireless ecosystem.

WIN encompasses eight regions within the United States: Pacific, Southwest, Rocky Mountain, Great Plains, Great Lakes, Northeast, Atlantic Coast and Southeast and also includes a provincial grassroots structure in Canada as well as a presence in the Bahamas.

Through the Wireless Industry Network program, NATE is committed to winning the day… all day, every day!
