Kitchen faucets are among the widely used items in a kitchen. Whether it be washing dishes, drinking water, or using water for cooking, you rely on the kitchen faucet to do it all. This is why one wrong decision can turn into a huge hassle. It is surprising that only a few people truly think about which kitchen faucet to buy.
There are quite a few options and types of faucets in the market. Each differs in terms of functionality, design, and price. To make sure you are able to derive most value out of the faucet, you must pick one wisely and take measures to equip yourself with the best kitchen faucets 2019 has.

Types of Kitchen Faucets

What a lot of people dont know is that there are different types of kitchen faucets. Some of the most common and acclaimed ones include the following.

Pull Out Faucets

This is the ideal type of faucet for those who have a small sink. When you are short on space, the last thing you need is your faucet taking up space when opened. This is why pull out faucets that turn on in the opposite direction of the sink might be perfect if you wish to use them to fill large pots with water.

Pull Down Faucets

These are the most well-known among the masses. They have been in use for generations and continue to be the most preferred type of faucet. This is mainly because of the theme they set for kitchens. Such a faucet features a high arch and looks much more royal than other types of kitchen faucets.
The amount of swiveling it can undergo depends on the specific model you invest in. Some may not swivel at all, while others can be turned 180 or 360 degrees. One issue with this type of faucet is that the water pressure is considerably less.

Fusion Faucets

This type of faucet is used in restaurant kitchens. When it comes to delivering high pressure, these faucets know no bounds. The pullout spray feature along with the high arch design allows such faucets to deliver the pros of both pull down and pull out faucets. Recently, manufacturers are also making home-friendly versions of these faucets.

Motion Detection Faucets

We live in a technologically advanced world, and a motion detection faucet reminds you of this fact time and again. These faucets are operated via battery and are activated automatically every time a cup/pot is under them or when you move your hands beneath them. Hence, you wont have to touch the faucet for it to work. Since it is a highly convenient and sophisticated technology, it
is quite pricey.

Two-Handle Faucets

This type of faucet has been on the market since forever. Now, it only exists because of its legacy rather than its functionality. Those who love the old times may continue to use it even though there are better options out there. The good thing about these faucets is that they remain functional even when one of the handles break. Most old houses already have this faucet preinstalled, leading to
its popular use.

How to Choose the Best Kitchen Faucets 2018

Selecting the best kitchen faucets 2018 has is not an easy task. There is no universal answer to the question, which is the best faucet? Instead, the perfect faucet for you depends on various factors. Here is how you should go about selecting a quality kitchen faucet.

Look at Your Sink

Before you go to the store and look at different options, you must check out your sink. Not every faucet is compatible with every sink. It all depends on the number of mounting holes available. Thus, when you go faucet hunting, check how many mounting holes it needs so that you dont buy incompatible items.

Compatibility with Water Lines

Seeing if your faucet is compatible with your sink is not the only compatibility factor you should be concerned about. It needs to be compatible with your water lines as well. For this, you will be required to look at the water line size as well the size of the shutoff valves. If you can afford it, you are better off with replacing your old shutoff valves when opting for a new kitchen faucet.

Select the Type of Kitchen Faucet and Finish

Since there are quite a variety of faucet designs in the market, making up your mind about which type of design to go for will make the task easy for you. Moreover, visualize how a given faucet will look with the rest of the kitchen once it has been installed. Do this especially if you care about aesthetics.

Set a Budget

Some kitchen faucets have a higher price than others. They have either better quality or better style. To ensure that you dont overspend, you must set a budget before you go to the store. Analyze how much you can invest in a kitchen faucet and filter your choices accordingly.
Dont opt for overly cheap or pricey products. It is best that you try to find a balance between the two. Look for extended warranties to make the purchase cost-effective for the long run.

Construction and Material

Faucets are made of different materials as well. The most popular and premium are brass and stainless steel. This is because not only do they look great, but they are also highly durable. However, if you are low on budget, you can always go for composite materials. You must also make sure that the construction is high quality. It should be lead-free and have a satisfactory flow rate. Look for these features in the product description. The valve quality should also be assessed. This is important to avoid regular instances of drips and leakages.
