Veteran's Day at Sizzler!

Öffentlich
· Gastgeber: Sizzler
Interessiert
  • clock
    Samstag, 11. November 2017 von 11:00 bis 16:00 PST
    Vor über einem Jahr
  • pin
    Participating Locations
Details
All Veteran’s and active duty military service personnel will receive a free lunch, from open until 4pm. They can choose from a 6oz steak entrée, single Malibu chicken entrée, or Crispy shrimp (6) entrée. Each of these three entrees includes a choice of side and a beverage on Saturday, November 11th, 2017 until 4pm.

Please contact your local Sizzler to see if they are participating!
