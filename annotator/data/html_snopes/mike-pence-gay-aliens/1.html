WASHINGTON (The Borowitz Report)—Making a serious announcement on the Pentagon on Thursday, Vice-President Mike Pence mentioned that the proposed United States Space Force was mandatory to defend the U.S. from homosexual aliens.
“I stand before you today to say that this country is under attack from outer-space gays,” Pence advised the navy gathering. “Only Space Force can protect us from their unimaginable evil.”
Pence detailed a nightmare situation by which “gay aliens by the thousands” land within the U.S. in “seemingly cute spacecraft” and “subvert life in America as we know it.”
“Let’s say, for example, that these gay aliens can assume human form,” he mentioned. “What’s to stop them from infiltrating normal bakeries and baking cakes for gay weddings?”
“I’ll tell you what’s to stop them,” he mentioned, pausing for dramatic impact. “Space Force.”
At the White House, CNN’s Jim Acosta requested the press secretary, Sarah Huckabee Sanders, how, precisely, Pence had developed his concept concerning the existence of homosexual aliens.
“No one is more qualified to talk about life on other planets than Mike Pence,” Sanders snapped.
