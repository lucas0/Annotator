Illegal Alien Denied Cops Service, “We Don’t Serve Pigs” [ENTITLED]

July 18, 2016 admin Uncategorized
It’s pretty sad to look around and see how police officers are being treated right now. They put their lives on the line every single day to defend our freedoms within the local community. We are more than thankful for our military, yet we fail to recognize the very people that keep us safe in our homes at night.
Police officers don’t get paid a lot either. They knowingly walk into possible gunfights almost every day. They do it because they heard the call to serve. They do it because they are true American patriots.
Well, there was just another case of disrespect against cops. In Alabama, last week a cashier decided that she didn’t want to serve the cops.
Maria Englesia pictured above told the officers to “get out of here” because they “didn’t serve pigs.” This was blatant.
McDonalds stepped in as soon as they heard what happened. They immediately fired the woman and issued a formal apology to the police department of Alabama.
Yet, it took one of the officer’s wives to complain before something was done.
While it’s great that McDonalds decided to step in and formally do the right thing, it shouldn’t have even come down to that. We need to value our police officers. That isn’t going to happen when everyday citizens decide to tell them to get lost.
In the end, it turns out that Maria was an illegal immigrant and not even eligible to be working. McDonalds has claimed responsibility for the hiring error and has “started a local investigation on all employees.” We won’t hold our breath.
If we are going to make true progress in this country, it’s going to come from building a new America. But, that’s the thing. This isn’t a “new” idea. All we need is the America of the Reagan years.
Remember letting your kids play outside without worrying? Remember those family dinners outside on the porch? Remember how the American flag rippled in the warm summer breeze as a testamanet to our country’s strength?
We remember. We desperately want to get back to a time like that when all Americans are grateful for the small things and do their part to add value to the community. The only way that’s going to happen is if we can start to show our police officers some respect and make their jobs easier. They have families as well and deserve to go home to their husbands and wives at night.
If you stand with us and agree that cops must be respected, please share this article. We must make a push to let our cops know they are values.
