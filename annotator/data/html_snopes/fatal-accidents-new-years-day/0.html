Did You Know?
View Archive
  • Collision with another motor vehicle in transport was the most common first harmful event for fatal crashes in 2017 (39 percent of all fatal crashes). Collisions with fixed objects and noncollisions accounted for 39 percent of fatal crashes.
    [Crashes 2017]
  • Traffic fatalities decreased by 2 percent from 2016 to 2017 for the Nation as a whole. Twenty-seven States showed decreases, ranging from 1 percent to as much as 25 percent.
    [States 2017]
  • The percent of alcohol-impaired driving fatalities has declined from 48 percent in 1982 to 29 percent in 2017.
    [Trends 2017]
