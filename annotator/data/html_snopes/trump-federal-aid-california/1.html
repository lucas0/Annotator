About

Salt Lake City Guardian is Salt Lake’s most trusted source for local news. With a focus on local content, the Guardian thrives to maintain a non-partisan newsroom making our content the most reliable source available in print and across the web. With an investigative team of 12 reporters and editors with a combined 195 years of experience we bring you the local news that matters.
Our team is eager to hear from you, so please feel free to reach out with any questions, comments, or criticisms.
For general editorial inquiries, please email editorial@saltlakecityguardian.com
For general subscription inquiries, please email subscriptions@saltlakecityguardian.com
