Italy. Province of Campania. Napoli. Harbour. Members of the Mutoid Waste Company on the deck of a commercial ship which will sail to Sicily. Using all types of industrial scrap for their raw materials the Mutoid Waste Company are a mobile and adaptable group of performance artists. Having the necessary equipment to allow them complete freedom, they have created an entirely new form of scultural-musical performance crossing the boundaries of circus, theatre and art.© 1999 Didier Ruef
