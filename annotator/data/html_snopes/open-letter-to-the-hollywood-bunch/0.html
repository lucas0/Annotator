CHARLIE DANIELS' VETERANS NON-PROFIT THE JOURNEY HOME PROJECT DONATES $25K TO THE SHEPHERD'S MEN

Nashville, Tenn. (May 30, 2019) — On Friday, May 24, country music legend Charlie Daniels' veterans non-profit The Journey Home Project (TJHP) held a special reception for The Shepherd's Men as the team of men journeyed through Nashville during a stop on its annual Shepherd's Run. The week-long, seven-city run (from Groton, CT to Atlanta, GA) raises awareness for troops with traumatic brain injuries and post-traumatic stress disorder.
