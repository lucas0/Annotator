How Much is Inside a Can of Shaving Cream?

How much shaving cream is in a can of shaving cream?

Hard to tell. Until now.

On Wednesday night, Mark, Leif and I emptied a can onto the kitchen table.

We used an 11 oz. can of Colgate shaving cream.

My home in Sacramento is about 20 feet above sea level.

The pile of shaving cream was pretty big, but not as big as we thought it would be.
