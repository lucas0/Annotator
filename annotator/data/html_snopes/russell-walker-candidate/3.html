Russell Walker (Republican) was a candidate who sought election to the North Carolina House of Representatives to represent District 48. Walker lost the general election on November 6, 2018, after advancing from the primary on May 8, 2018.

Biography

Walker earned his bachelor's degree from the Stevens Institute of Technology in 1964. His professional experience includes working as a chemical engineer.[1]
