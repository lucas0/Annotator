South Africa's Great White Shark, Peschak & Scholl


South Africa's Great White Shark by Thomas Peschak & Michael Scholl this recently published book contains Award winning Great White photographs showcasing their behaviour.

The essential guide for surfers, scuba divers, kayakers, swimmers and fishermen - in fact anyone venturing into the Ocean.
An informative text for divers, marine biologists, guides and photographers, providing in-depth coverage of Great White behaviour, ecology, conservation and eco-tourism
The facts behind cage diving, chumming and shark bites.

An informative, educational read helping to dispel many of the myths surrounding the incredible Great Whites. eg: 'Great Whites are not as dangerous as we are lead to believe. Over the last 83 years there have been only 22 fatalities attributed to Great White bites in South African waters.'
